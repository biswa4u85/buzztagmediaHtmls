Other Important Points

  • If a player is unable to start the match due to any reason but is announced in the starting seven, he/she will not score any points. However the replacement (player) will earn points for the match (including starting points).
  • Substitute appearance points will only be rewarded when the player enter the field of play for the first time.
  • Super Tackle points will be added to the successful tackle points (20 pts for successful tackle + 8 pts for super tackle).
  • Raid touch points will be given to the raider for every opponent he gets out or puts out.
  • Super 10 points will be given to each player only once during a match.
  • Points for getting the entire team (opposition) all out will only be given to players who are a part of the starting 7.
  • Negative points for getting all out will only be given to players who are part of the starting 7.
  • If a player receives any card, he will continue to get negative (for 'Own Team All Out') and positive (for 'Getting Opposition All Out') points.
  • Points deducted for Red Cards include Yellow Card and Green Card deductions. Similarly, points deducted for Yellow Cards include deductions for Green Cards.
  • If someone in the team/support receives a Green/Yellow/Red card, it will not have any impact on the players' individual points.
  • Any event during extra time will be considered for awarding points.
  • Any event during Golden Raids will not be considered for awarding points.
  • A player who has been transferred from one team to the other might still be available for selection for his older team until the next scheduled team update on Notout11. However, no points will be ascribed to the player in such a situation.
  • A player who has not partaken in the game as part of the starting 7 or as a substitute will not get negative points for receiving yellow/red/green cards for off-field activities.
  • Once a match has been deemed as completed and winners have been declared, no further adjustments will be made to the points awarded. Points awarded for a LIVE game will be subject to change only when the match status is “In Progress” or “In Review”.

  • A user gains points based on the prediction they make for each ball of the chosen over.
  • Different points are assigned for different possible events for every ball in an over.
  • These events comprise a wide range of outcomes ranging from a dot ball all the way to the nature of dismissal/wicket.
  • A right estimation/guess would be rewarded with the points allotted to the run/wicket.
  • A wrong prediction would result in a deduction from the gross total according to points allotted to the run/wicket.
  • Points for Spot Fantasy are only valid for all the legal deliveries during a cricket match. No points are allotted for extras i.e. wide ball, no ball, leg bye..
