What is Fantasy Football?

A game that requires users to create their fantasy teams based on definite standards from a group of players scheduled to compete in the live football match on the given day. Participants usually need to pay an entry fee to enter a fantasy contest. Once you have deposited the administrative fee charged by the platform operator, you can draft your team and enter the desired contest. The entry fee is typically pooled for distribution among the platform users depending on their performance. Based on that, the users are rewarded points. Users gain points according to the score sheet and the performance of their selected players (fantasy team) in the actual match.
