ABOUT US – Notout11

GODLINE GAMING PVT LTD is India's most authentic and trusted fantasy sports platform. Its unique and innovative product offering has been successful in aligning with its exponentially growing user base. It is a game of skill that offers cricket, football and kabaddi fans a platform to showcase their fantasy knowledge. We facilitate the best User-Interface and User Experience, which helps the individual to earn some handsome amount of money with their gaming knowledge. We always make sure that our users get the smoothest experience while taking part in fantasy sports leagues. Here, fans can create their own virtual team of real-life players for upcoming matches; score points based on their on-field performance and compete with other fans. After the successful signing of Gautam Gambhir and Ravinder Pahal, we recently roped in Indian women star cricketer Taniya Bhatia as the face of our organisation. With her comprehensive understanding and astute vision, the leading fantasy sports operator strives to reach the pinnacle of fantasy sports.

Started as a platform solely dedicated to cricket leagues, Notout11 has now shifted to other sports as well. Now you can enjoy fantasy football/kabaddi on our platform. Draft your teams, join from a wide range of available contests and compete across a variety of cricket, football and kabaddi leagues. From small domestic leagues to every major tournament, Notout11 allows the users to take part in every match. Notout11 helps sports fans to connect deeper with the sport they love by being an owner of the team, not just a spectator. It is a great platform for cricket, football and kabaddi fans who want to make the best use of the comprehensive understanding of the game, and become the part of the biggest sports community. Besides being a leader in the fantasy sports industry, we also have other fun filled online games such as Solitaire, Carrom and Bubble Shooter.

Our aim is to provide the best fantasy sports platform to our users and build a sports community where people can share their ideas. Currently, we are limited to only cricket, football and kabaddi fantasy games but soon, we’ll increase our bandwidth by including other sports. We are targeting to build a strong user base and maintain excellent service for our users. We are understanding the market needs and setting our goals accordingly. Notout11 understands its users and serves them with the best features to enhance their experience with us.
