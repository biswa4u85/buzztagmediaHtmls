What is fantasy cricket?

Fantasy cricket is a game of skill where you can create your fantasy teams in the fantasy cricket leagues. In fantasy cricket league, you make your virtual team of 11 players from the set of 22-25 players of the upcoming real-life matches. Based on the performance of your team’s players, you will be ranked in the joined contests and win real cash accordingly.

As a fantasy team selector, your motive is to pick those 11 players who can perform well during the match and earn valuable points for your fantasy team. Before creating your fantasy team, it’s advised to do some research about the recent form of players and then build your strongest team. Always be unique with your ideas and listen to your intuition. Other than researching, there are many factors which you should consider before creating your fantasy cricket teams.
